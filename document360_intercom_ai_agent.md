# Connecting Document360 to Intercom as an AI Agent

Integrating Document360 with Intercom using eesel AI enables a seamless connection that allows for automated, intelligent responses within Intercom conversations. This guide walks you through the process of setting up Document360 as a knowledge base for eesel AI, which in turn, serves as an AI agent in Intercom.

## 1. Find and Install eesel AI App

Begin by locating the eesel AI application within the Intercom App Store and click "Install now" to add it to your Intercom environment.

![Find and Install eesel AI](https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/968291610/ec2faed821fbf71060c4672a/image.png)

## 2. Complete OAuth Flow

Follow the OAuth authentication flow to grant eesel AI the necessary permissions to access your Intercom account.

![OAuth Flow](https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/968292359/f4ec34c95cf3c3530f3fc501/image.png)

## 3. Sign Up for an eesel AI Account

If you're new to eesel AI, you'll need to create an account. After installing the app and completing the OAuth flow, sign up with your email and password.

![Sign Up](https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/968293722/c520f208423953f90c76ce4d/image.png)

## 4. Add Knowledge from Document360

To enable eesel AI to provide accurate responses based on your documentation, add your Document360 knowledge base as a source.

1. Navigate to the "Add Knowledge" section within eesel AI.
2. Choose to add knowledge manually or through a scraping process if your Document360 content is publicly accessible.

![Add Knowledge](https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/968295557/bd6fadb927d7995feca02458/image.png)

You will see a progress indicator as eesel AI crawls and indexes your Document360 pages.

![Knowledge Crawling](https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/968296166/7ebd772233d0caca3055cf11/image.png)

## 5. Chat with Your AI Agent

Test the integration by chatting with your eesel AI agent. This step ensures that eesel AI can accurately retrieve and provide information from your Document360 knowledge base.

![Chat with AI](https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/968297804/2af192f403192a53da19cb4b/image.png)

## 6. Configure Intercom Integration

Fine-tune how eesel AI interacts with your Intercom setup:

- Go to the eesel AI dashboard and select "Intercom" from the left navigation panel.
- Adjust settings such as response type (internal or external note), trigger conditions (e.g., reply to all conversations or only when @mentioned), and more.

![Configure Intercom](https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/973772701/2799b790959ccddcc9b8d111/image.png)

### Optional Configurations

- **Internal Note Settings:** Decide if eesel should automatically leave internal notes for any reply or only under specific conditions.

![Internal Note Settings](https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/973775095/8d5833e3bdeddd959cefef21/image.png)

- **Human Handover:** Configure automatic handover to human agents if eesel AI is unable to address the query effectively.

![Human Handover](https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/973787947/6f3a4f97afa6c80b137c49b4/image.png)
